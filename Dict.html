Dict.js – a simple dict.json front-end.

Look up any word from the definition simply by clicking it.

Use the back and forward browser buttons to move through recent definitions.

Credits

Written using mootools and FancyForm.

IE history support based on a jQuery history plugin, originally developed by Taku Sano (Mikage Sawatari), forked by Takayuki Miwa.

Definition layout heavily inspired by Apple's Dictionary.app.
